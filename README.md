# 📊 Ecommerce Sales Analysis using Pandas & Plotly

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** of the Sample Superstore dataset using **Pandas** and **Plotly**. The objective is to analyze sales performance, profit trends, customer segments, and product categories to generate meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Perform data cleaning and preprocessing.
- Analyze monthly sales and profit trends.
- Identify top-performing product categories and sub-categories.
- Compare sales and profit across customer segments.
- Calculate and visualize the Sales-to-Profit Ratio.
- Generate business insights from the dataset.

## 📌 Key Business Insights

- Technology category generated the highest overall sales.
- Customer segments contributed differently to revenue and profit.
- Monthly sales and profit showed noticeable seasonal variations.
- Some sub-categories achieved high sales but comparatively lower profitability.
- Sales-to-Profit Ratio helped identify the most efficient customer segments.
